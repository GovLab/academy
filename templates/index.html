Empowering civic entrepreneurs to tackle public problems that touch real people’s lives

Delivering efficient services, designing effective policies, and addressing tough public problems with legitimacy often require active, technology-enabled citizen engagement. This is true for all levels of government – and for NGOs and businesses as well. Indeed, successful public innovation depends on collaboration with those outside the normal institutional walls. New opinions are, of course, helpful. But so is ready access to the expertise and elbow grease of a wider range of people.

GovLab Academy offers:

  • Access through the Library to high-quality, curated content aimed at busy practitioners
  • Mentoring designed to help take public interest projects from idea to implementation
  • Connections to subject matter experts, who know what’s been tried and what does and does not work
  • Tools for peer-to-peer learning and skill sharing
